Elera Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Information We Collect

Elera may collect and store limited information required for its operation, including Discord User IDs, Server IDs, Channel IDs, Role IDs, and bot settings.

How Information Is Used

Collected information is used solely to provide and improve Elera's features and functionality.

Data Sharing

Elera does not sell, trade, or share collected information with third parties, except when required by law.

Data Storage

Data is stored only as long as necessary for the operation of the bot.

User Rights

Server owners may request the removal of server-related data by contacting the developers.

Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect stored information, but no system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of Elera constitutes acceptance of any changes.

Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the Elera development team.
